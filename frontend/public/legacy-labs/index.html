Welcome!

This platform is an extracurricular resource for COMP3423 Human Computer Interaction, developed by a research team led by Dr. Jeff Tang and Prof. Johan Hoorn.

Expected Outcomes

Through interactive experiences with Fitts' Law, Weber's Law, etc., you will better understand of HCI principles.
By practicing with typical cases, you will improve your skills in interface design and user experience optimization.
By applying HCI theoretical knowledge to practical problems, you will enhance your ability to solve real-world issues.

Participation Notes

  1. The performance in the labs will not directly affect your grades. Please avoid consulting external resources.
  2. You will receive additional course grade points upon completing all labs and surveys.
  3. A complete experience takes approximately 20 minutes.
  4. Please complete all experiences and submit the surveys by 23:59 on December 5, 2025.

Instructions for Use

  1. Use latest Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. Avoid Safari.
  2. Ensure a stable network connection.
  3. Report issues to yandi.lu@connect.polyu.hk.
