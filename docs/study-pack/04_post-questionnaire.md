# Post-Session Questionnaire Battery (participant-facing)

> Administered once, after all four topics and the AI-tutor step. Three short instruments:
> motivation (IMI), interaction with the game and AI tutor (CoI, reworded), and satisfaction
> (ARCS). About **28 items, ~8 minutes**.
>
> **One response scale throughout.** For every statement, mark how much you agree:
>
> `1 = Strongly disagree   2 = Disagree   3 = Neither agree nor disagree   4 = Agree   5 = Strongly agree`
>
> There are no right answers — we want your honest impression. Answer every item.
> **No scoring marks appear here.** Subscale membership and reverse-scored items are in
> `06_scoring-codebook-analysis.md` (researcher-only).

**Participant code:** `P___`

When you read "the activities", think of the whole COMPGame experience today — the Understanding
games, the Assessment games, the quizzes, and the AI tutor together.
